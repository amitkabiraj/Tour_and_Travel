feature location

Mayapur

Mayapur ISKCON Temple is a beautiful and peaceful place. It's in West Bengal, India. People from all over the world visit to pray and learn about spiritual teachings. The temple has stunning architecture and serene gardens, making it a truly special place for worship and reflection..

Hajarduyari

Murshidabad's Hazarduari Palace, also known as Jahangir Palace, is a grand historical building in West Bengal, India. It has a thousand doors, hence the name. This palace witnessed significant events during the Mughal era and offers visitors a glimpse into the region's rich heritage and royal past.

Jiaganj

Katgola Bagan in Jiaganj, West Bengal, is a picturesque garden with a fascinating history. It's known for its ancient trees and tranquil atmosphere. Visitors can explore the lush greenery and learn about the legacy of the Tagore family, who played a significant role in Bengal's cultural heritage.
